Frequently Asked Questions

Aapke mann mein uthne wale har sawal ka jawab yahan hai.

1. Can I get job-ready skills from the courses offered at HIIT Ambala?
+

Answer: Yes, definitely! HIIT Ambala ka curriculum industry experts ne design kiya hai. Hum sirf theory nahi padhate, balki students ko live projects aur latest tools par training dete hain taaki course khatam hote hi aap ek professional ki tarah kaam shuru kar sakein.

2. Are internships included in the courses at HIIT Ambala?
+

Answer: Haan, hamare zyada tar premium courses mein 3 se 6 mahine ki internship shamil hai. Is dauran aap asli companies ke projects par kaam karte hain, jisse aapko workplace ka experience milta hai aur aapka resume strong banta hai.

3. Are weekend classes available for working professionals?
+

Answer: Bilkul! Hum jaante hain ki job ke saath padhna mushkil hota hai, isliye hum Saturday aur Sunday ko special batches chalate hain. Ye batches working professionals ke liye design kiye gaye hain taaki unki job par asar na pade.

4. Does HIIT Ambala provide 100% job assistance?
+

Answer: Hum 100% placement support dete hain. Hamari dedicated team aapke Mock Interviews karwayegi, Resume banwayegi aur aapko hamare 150+ hiring partners (Mahindra, Wipro etc.) ke paas interviews ke liye bhejegi jab tak aapka selection nahi ho jata.

5. Are the courses at HIIT Ambala practical and hands-on?
+

Answer: Hamari training 80% practical hai. Hum "Learning by Doing" mein believe karte hain. Students ko hamari high-tech labs mein rozana practice karne ka mauka milta hai taaki software aur hardware par unka haath saaf ho sake.

6. What kind of companies hire students from HIIT Ambala?
+

Answer: Hamare students top MNCs aur tech companies mein placed hain. Isme IT firms, manufacturing companies aur design studios shamil hain. Aap upar hamare 'Prime Recruiters' section mein unke logos dekh sakte hain.
